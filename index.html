Baramulla micro-Climate Strategy

Baramulla experiences crisp, cold winters with snow, and warm, pleasant summers. Gardening here requires plants that demand winter dormancy and can handle intense mountain sun.

Winter Prep

Heavy mulching is critical by late November to protect root zones from deep freezes.

Spring Winds

Elevated beds and tall plants need staking by April to prevent wind damage.

Summer Hydration

Sloped beds drain fast. Deep, infrequent watering in July/August builds strong roots.
