Weekly Operations

  • Deep Watering (Slope): Check soil moisture 2 inches deep. Water early morning.
  • Deadheading: Remove spent blooms from Wall Pocket perennials to force secondary blooming.
  • Weed Eradication: Inspect slope borders. Hand-pull weeds before roots establish in stone crevices.
  • Pest Recon: Check undersides of Hosta and Rose leaves for aphid/slug activity.

Seasonal Baramulla Protocols

Spring (March - May)

Apply slow-release NPK fertilizer to all trees and wall pockets. Begin prophylactic organic neem oil spraying. Clear winter debris.

Summer (June - August)

Increase irrigation frequency for slope. Install stakes for tall Delphiniums. Refresh organic mulch layers to retain moisture.

Autumn (Sept - Nov)

Harvest Saffron Crocus. Divide overgrown perennials (Iris, Hostas). Rake Chinar leaves for compost generation.

Winter (Dec - Feb)

Execute hard pruning on dormant deciduous trees. Protect sensitive crowns with thick pine needle mulch against heavy snow loads.
